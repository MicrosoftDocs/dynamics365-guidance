The business process catalog assigns unique IDs to each end-to-end scenario and the related child entries that support that end-to-end scenario. On the Microsoft Learn site, we use the IDs to list the end-to-end scenarios, but we don't use the IDs of the related business process areas, business processes, scenarios, system processes, and test cases.
