# Real-world case studies

The case studies in this section show how organizations use Dynamics 365 to create innovative, real-world solutions.

Each case study provides an in-depth look at how organizations build applications, automated workflows, and conversational agents using Dynamics 365. These stories provide valuable insights into how customers solve complex challenges, streamline processes, and achieve their goals. For citizen developers and solution architects, case studies serve as inspiration and practical guidance, showcasing the potential of Dynamics 365 in various scenarios.

## Benefits of reading case studies

- **Learn from real-world examples**: Case studies offer a glimpse into how other organizations tackled similar challenges. By reading about their experiences, you can gain a better understanding of practical implementations of applications and agents and how Dynamics 365 can help organizations solve complex problems.

- **Discover best practices**: Each case study highlights the strategies and practices that led to successful outcomes. Use these insights to adopt proven approaches and avoid common pitfalls in your own projects.

- **Understand the impact**: Many case studies include measurable results, such as efficiency gains, cost savings, and higher customer satisfaction. These metrics help you build a strong business case for your initiatives.

- **Get inspired**: Innovative solutions from other organizations can spark new ideas. Whether you're looking to automate processes, improve customer experiences, or drive digital transformation, these case studies can help you identify new opportunities.
