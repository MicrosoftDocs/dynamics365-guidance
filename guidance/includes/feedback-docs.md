On the *learn.microsoft.com* website, each documentation article encourages you to provide feedback. We've changed the mechanism a couple of times based on, well, feedback. Currently, we ask you to choose the **thumbs up/thumbs down** action in the right rail next to the article. You can choose a reason for your rating from a list of predefined reasons based on your choice of thumbs up/down, and write a comment if you want to. The feedback goes straight to the article's author and the team that owns the docs.
