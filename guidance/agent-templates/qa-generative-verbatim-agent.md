# Q&A Generative and Verbatim Responses Agent

Enables safe adoption of generative AI in regulatory and sensitive scenarios by using AI to understand customer questions and match knowledge articles while returning exact, unmodified content—eliminating hallucination risks.

## Overview

Copilot Studio agent leveraging generative AI for intelligent question understanding and semantic matching to knowledge base but returns unmodified verbatim content from source articles. AI interprets user intent and finds relevant knowledge articles using advanced NLU, then delivers exact approved text without summarization, paraphrasing, or generation—ensuring regulatory compliance and eliminating hallucination concerns.

## Key benefits

- AI-powered question interpretation with exact verbatim responses
- Zero hallucination risk—responses are as-is from approved knowledge articles
- Maintains regulatory compliance with precise, approved language
- Semantic search matching questions to relevant knowledge base content
- Safe Gen AI adoption in sensitive use cases (tax, legal, policy)
- Citation and source tracking for audit trails
- Approved content delivery without AI modification or summarization
